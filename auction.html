How to buy a Car from

Live Auction

STEP-1

You can select any car from auction-research service of live auction. Just tell us about your selected car name and lot number. We will confirm bid for you in auction as well.

STEP-2

Once you have decided which vehicle you want, kindly send us the auction lot number. We will give you the transaction of condition and estimate cost. We will place bidding on the car up to your budget Before we bid, we'd like a deposit of approx. 40% of your budget which is refundable if we are unable to win the bid.

STEP-3

After the invoice issued, please make the payment through Cash/Cheque/ TT/RPGS, then we open a LC in our bank. All the payments will be recorded.

STEP-4

We will give you the details about the booking of your car and shipping information. After the car loaded and shipped to our port, Invice, Bill of lading and the other necessary original documents exporter will be sent to the our Bank by air service or courier services.

STEP-5

After landing of the car in our Chattagram/ Mongla port then you should make payment of customs duty, Vat & taxes, port charges and clearing agent charges and also pay to us driver-octan road cost.

STEP-6

After paid to customs duty, Vat & Taxes then Car will realese from the port and asusually car will come to our showroom.

STEP-7

When Car arrived at showroom then require more one or two days to ready of the car. We will checkup the car exterior and interior. Will change Engine oil, Air Fielter, Mobil Fielter, AC Fielter Etc.

STEP-8

Last step is completeion of Vehicle registration & insurance. If you have Bank loan procedures then you should be completeion of all neccessary documetation as per bank requirements. You should be completeion of the all neccessary works in bank before delivery of the Car.

Follow all the step carefully
